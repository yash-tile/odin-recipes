Fish Curry

Description

Savor the rich flavors of Indian-style Fish Curry, a delightful dish featuring tender fish in a fragrant and spicy curry sauce. Serve with steamed rice or naan for a complete meal.

Ingredients

  • 500 grams fish fillets (any white fish)
  • 2 large onions, finely chopped
  • 3 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1/4 cup cooking oil
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Clean and cut fish fillets into bite-sized pieces. Marinate with yogurt, turmeric, and salt for 30 minutes.
  2. In a pan, heat oil and saut√© chopped onions until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until the raw smell disappears.
  4. Stir in curry powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, and red chili powder. Cook for a minute to release the flavors.
  5. Add tomato puree and cook until the oil separates from the masala.
  6. Add marinated fish pieces and cook until they are cooked through. Adjust salt and spice levels to taste.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or naan.
