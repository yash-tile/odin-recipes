Chicken Biryani

Description

Dive into the flavors of Chicken Biryani, a classic Indian dish that combines fragrant basmati rice, tender chicken, and aromatic spices. A delightful one-pot meal for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice, soaked
  • 500 grams chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1/4 cup cooking oil or ghee
  • 2 tablespoons biryani masala
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup chopped mint leaves
  • 1/2 cup chopped coriander leaves
  • Whole spices: bay leaves, cardamom, cloves, cinnamon
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Wash and soak basmati rice for 30 minutes.
  2. In a large pot, heat oil or ghee. Add sliced onions and sauté until golden brown. Remove half for garnishing.
  3. Add whole spices and ginger-garlic paste to the pot. Sauté until fragrant.
  4. Add chicken pieces and cook until browned. Stir in biryani masala, yogurt, mint, and coriander leaves.
  5. Drain soaked rice and add it to the pot. Mix well and add water (1:2 ratio of rice to water).
  6. Cover and cook on low heat until rice is tender and chicken is cooked through (about 20 minutes).
  7. Garnish with fried onions and serve hot. Enjoy your Chicken Biryani!
