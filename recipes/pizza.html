Pizza

Description

Enjoy the simplicity of a Classic Margherita Pizza with fresh tomatoes, mozzarella cheese, and basil leaves. This timeless pizza is a perfect blend of flavors.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 8 ounces fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 2-3 ripe tomatoes, thinly sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust.
  4. Place slices of fresh mozzarella and tomato evenly on the sauce.
  5. Drizzle with extra-virgin olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  7. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.
  8. Top with fresh basil leaves before serving.
